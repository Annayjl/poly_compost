Our Mission

Our ultimate goal is to reduce on food waste by uniting community and even local businesses to redirect their compostables and away from the landfill and , instead, use those nutrients to build nutrient-rich soil and even build a soil conditioner to enhance soil. reducing on food waste is essential in building healthy soil and fighting climate change.
