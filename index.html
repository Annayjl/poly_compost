What is composting?

Composting is the natural procedure of decomposing and recycling of organic goods into a nutritious rich soil also known as compost as a result the proposed solution to this matter is a compost bin which the designed will be small enough for any household to own one. We decided to introduce mini compost bins to encourage people to start composting to reduce food wastage at their homes and reduce their carbon foot-steps to help the earth.
